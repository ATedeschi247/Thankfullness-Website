Be Thankful!

We should all be thankful for what we have! Even as the holidays approach, and we think about what we should get ourselves for Christmas, we must remember to stop and be thankful for what we already have.


For instance, I am grateful for:

  • The people around me, like my friends and family.
  • The things we often take for granted, like the roof over my head and the food on my table.
  • The good school that I go to (even if I do feel that I hate it at times).
  • The more superfluous stuff, like my phone, TV, video games, etc.

An image of friends. Get it?
